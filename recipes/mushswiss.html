Mushroom N Swiss Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

A mushroom swiss burger is a mountain of a burger that can fill up those with the biggest of appetites. This ooey gooey umami bomb that you have seen at many fast-food places can now be made for you and your family.

Ingredients you need:

  • 1 pound of lean ground beef
  • 1 cup of thinly sliced mushroom
  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 clove of minced garlic
  • 4 hamburger bread
  • Some salt
  • Some black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of chili flakes
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of italian seasoning
  • 4 slices of swiss cheese

Steps

  1. PIn a medium bowl combine ground beef, salt and pepper, steak seasoning (or alternate seasonings) and mix together well so that all seasonings are incorporated.
  2. Divide mixture into 4 equal parts and shape each into a patty.
  3. n a small-medium pan or skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add mushrooms, garlic, and Italian seasoning and saute until mushrooms are very tender and garlic is fragrant. Remove from heat, add a layer of swiss cheese, cover the pan with foil to keep the heat in, and set aside.
  4. Grill burger patties over medium heat for about 5-6 minutes on each side or until desired doneness is reached.
  5. Top patties with a slice of swiss cheese each and allow to sit on the grill (lid closed) for 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melty.
  6. Assemble burgers by arranging any desired toppings on the bottom half of each bun, then following up with the cheesy burger patty, and topping with the mushrooms and top half of the bun. Serve immediately.

Bon Appetit!
